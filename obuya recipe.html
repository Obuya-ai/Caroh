RECIPE OF FRIED MEAT

INGREDIENTS

    1. Boiled chopped pieces of meat
    2. Diced tomatoes
    3. Chopped onions
    4. Chopped capsicum
    5.A pinch of salt
    6.Cooking oil
    7. Curry powder

PROCEDURE

    1. Light the fire
    2. Put the sufuria and add cooking oil to heat
    3. Add the chopped onions and fry until golden brown
    4.Add diced tomatoes
    5. Add the chopped capsicum
    6. Add a pinch of salt to taste, stir until a thick paste is formed
    7. Add curry powder to the paste and stir to mix well
    8. Add the pieces of meat as you stir until it mixes well with the paste
    9. Leave under a low heat until it cooks well
    10. Serve hot with vegetables and ugali or rice
